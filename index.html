OUT OF FUEL

Out of fuel is a challenging game on mobile platforms. The main challenge is to drive a car with low fuel to reach the gas station before it runs out of fuel ,including additional challenges while playing, such as obstacles , thorns , barrels and tornados.

About US

Barka Studio was founded by Abdulaziz Barakat in 2023. Abdulaziz is the owner and has been working as an indie game developer in all aspects of the games industry (Programming, Modeling, UI Design & Art, Music). Out of fuel is the first game by the studio and there are also some upcoming games. We will declare them through social accounts. The studio plans to make high quality games with new ideas for mobile platforms and then expand to publish on consoles (PlayStation/Xbox/Nintendo).
The studio is currently located in Saudi Arabia and we are planning to have many members from worldwide and in other countries too.
